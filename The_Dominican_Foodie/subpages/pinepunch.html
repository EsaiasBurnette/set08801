PINEAPPLE PUNCH RECIPE

Pineapple punch is a refreshing and tropical drink that is perfect for hot summer days. To make this delicious drink, chunks of ripe pineapple are blended until smooth and then combined with water and sugar in a saucepan. The mixture is simmered over low heat for several minutes and then strained to remove any solids. Lime juice and rum are added to the mixture before chilling it in the refrigerator. Once the punch is cold, it is served over ice cubes and garnished with a pineapple wedge or a sprig of mint. This sweet and tangy beverage is a crowd-pleaser and can be enjoyed with or without alcohol.

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe pineapple
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 cup rum (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Cut the pineapple into chunks, removing the skin and core.
  2. Place the pineapple chunks in a blender or food processor and puree until smooth.
  3. In a large saucepan, combine the pineapple puree, water, and sugar. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally to dissolve the sugar.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and strain the mixture through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any solids.
  6. Stir in the lime juice and rum, if using.
  7. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or until cold.
  8. Serve the pineapple punch over ice cubes and garnish with a pineapple wedge or a sprig of mint, if desired.
