CALLALOO SOUP RECIPE

Callaloo soup is a traditional dish from the Caribbean, particularly popular in the Commonwealth of Dominica. This soup is made with two types of leafy greens, callaloo leaves and taro or dasheen leaves, which are sautéed with onion and garlic and then simmered in chicken or vegetable broth and coconut milk. The soup is seasoned with salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and thyme leaves, and then pureed until smooth. Chopped scallions and lemon juice are added to the soup before serving. This soup is not only delicious, but it is also packed with nutrients and health benefits. Callaloo leaves are rich in vitamins and minerals, while coconut milk is high in healthy fats and has been linked to a range of health benefits.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh callaloo leaves (or substitute spinach)
  • 1 pound taro or dasheen leaves (or substitute kale)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme leaves
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the callaloo and taro/dasheen leaves into small pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until soft, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the chopped callaloo and taro/dasheen leaves to the pot and stir well.
  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and coconut milk, then add the salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and thyme leaves. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 30-35 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Remove the pot from heat and let cool for a few minutes.
  7. Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth.
  8. Return the soup to the pot and reheat over medium heat.
  9. Add the chopped scallions and lemon juice, and stir well.
  10. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Add hot sauce if desired.
  11. Serve hot with a slice of crusty bread.
