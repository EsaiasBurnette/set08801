Nature's Healing Secrets - Discover Dominica's Natural Remedies

Discover the healing power of Dominica's natural remedies recipes, crafted from the island's bountiful natural resources. Our remedies have been used for centuries by our ancestors, and are a true reflection of the island's connection to nature. From healing teas made with locally grown herbs to soothing balms made from tropical plants, our remedies offer a holistic approach to wellness. At the heart of our remedies is the concept of "bush medicine," a traditional practice that blends the healing power of nature with cultural beliefs and practices. With locally grown ingredients like lemongrass, ginger, and soursop, our remedies can help with everything from digestive issues to respiratory ailments

ILLNESSES AND THEIR BUSH MEDICINES

Soursop Leaf Tea - Immune System Booster

Soursop leaves are commonly used in Dominica to make a tea that is believed to have many health benefits. It is said to boost the immune system, reduce inflammation, and improve digestion.

Fever Grass Tea - Fever and Cold Treatment

Fever grass (lemongrass) is another popular herb used in Dominica to make tea. It is believed to have anti-inflammatory properties and is commonly used to treat fever and colds.

Guava Leaf Tea - Blood Pressure Regulator

Guava leaves are used to make a tea that is believed to have many health benefits. It is said to help lower blood pressure, reduce inflammation, and promote healthy skin.
