Drink in the Flavors of Paradise - Discover Dominica's Beverages

Indulge in the refreshing and exotic flavors of Dominica's beverages, crafted with the island's natural bounty. Our drinks are a reflection of our lush tropical landscapes, with ingredients sourced from the island's fertile soils and pristine waters. Sip on a tangy and sweet sorrel drink, made from the hibiscus flower, or enjoy a refreshing glass of coconut water straight from the tree. These local beverage recipe will be sure to make you feel like your in the nature isle.

BEVERAGES

Pineapple Punch

Fresh pineapple is blended with sugar, water, and spices such as cinnamon and nutmeg to make a sweet and refreshing drink. The punch is typically served chilled over ice.

Rum Punch

A classic Caribbean drink, rum punch is made with rum, fruit juice, and sugar. In Dominica, the drink is often made with local fruits such as passionfruit and guava.
