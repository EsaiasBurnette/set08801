RUM PUNCH RECIPE

Rum punch is a classic Caribbean cocktail that is beloved in the Commonwealth of Dominica. This refreshing drink is made by mixing pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, grenadine syrup, and white rum together in a pitcher. The addition of ice cubes and orange slices makes for a perfectly chilled and visually appealing drink. Rum punch is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a summer barbecue or a beachside picnic. The tropical flavors and boozy kick are sure to transport you to the warm sands and crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup grenadine syrup
  • 1 cup white rum
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slices, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pitcher, mix together the pineapple juice, orange juice, lime juice, and grenadine syrup.
  2. Add the white rum and stir well to combine.
  3. Add ice cubes to the pitcher and stir again.
  4. Pour the rum punch into glasses and garnish with orange slices.
  5. Serve the rum punch immediately and enjoy!
